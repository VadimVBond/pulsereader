A focused design system reduces drift between layouts and speeds up implementation. Use content-first primitives: cards, metadata rows, and typography tokens.

## What to Standardize

- Heading scales and spacing rhythm.
- Card surfaces, borders, and states.
- Shared navigation and footer patterns.
- Accessible color tokens for both themes.
