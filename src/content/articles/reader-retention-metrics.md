Track scroll depth, return frequency, and completion rate to understand whether readers finish and revisit your content.

## Metrics to Watch

- Completion rate by article length.
- Repeat visits over 7 and 30 days.
- Time to first meaningful scroll.
- Share-to-read ratio for discovery channels.
